Kazakhstan,[c] officially the Republic of Kazakhstan,[d] is a transcontinental country located mainly in Central Asia and partly in Eastern Europe.[e] It borders Russia to the north and west, China to the east, Kyrgyzstan to the southeast, Uzbekistan to the south, and Turkmenistan to the southwest, with a coastline along the Caspian Sea. Its capital is Astana, known as Nur-Sultan from 2019 to 2022. Almaty, Kazakhstan's largest city, was the country's capital until 1997.
